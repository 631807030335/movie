I prefer science fiction movies and comedy movies

Comedy movies

The style of comedy is humorous, relaxed and cheerful. The audience not only meets the requirements of entertainment, but also receives the corresponding ideological education. Comedies pay attention to novel and subtle ideas, and pay attention to the use of misunderstanding, coincidence, exaggeration and satire. And through the character's funny action and witty dialogue, to shape the comic character and form a comic artistic style..

Fiction movies

Fantasy films will show concern for political or social issues, as well as philosophical aspects such as the human situation. Some science fiction films are adapted from science fiction works, but science fiction films will focus on the literary or humanistic elements, ignoring the scientific rigor and logic that science fiction pays more attention to. The two can not be compared. The same standard can not be applied to science fiction and science fiction films at the same time..
